Customizing / Paid support

Preliminary Remark: This section deals about paid services. For free help please post your request in the forum section.

If you decided to use phpMyFAQ or even use it on your daily business but need a customizing then you might want to dicuss it your system adminstrator, your IT consultant - or with us! Customization could mean:

  • modifying the existing system according to your needs
  • enhancing the system with new features
  • adapting and integrating your existing website and applications

Please send us an email to support@phpmyfaq.de if you're interested.
